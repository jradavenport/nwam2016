    Parking
    Parking is available in the nearby C or 12A lots, just south of the Academic West building. Parking is free on Saturdays.

    Travel
    Bellingham, a city of 80,000, is located 59 miles south of Vancouver, British Columbia, and 90 miles north of Seattle along I-5. The campus overlooks Bellingham Bay and is an hour drive from the snow-capped Cascade Mountains.

    For those not able to drive directly to WWU, here are a few alternative transportation options to reach Bellingham:
    • Bellingham International Airport, serviced by Allegiant Air and Alaska/Horizon Airlines.
    • County Bus: (360) 676-7433, Bellingham/Whatcom County bus system via Whatcom Transportation Authority (WTA).
    • Bolt Bus
    • Amtrak (800) 872-7245, Amtrak departs from Fairhaven (south Bellingham) traveling south through Seattle and north through Vancouver, BC.
    • More options for transportation are discussed here. If you have questions or concerns about traveling to NWAM 2016, please let us know!
